#   CN-02: LAN vs WAN

1) Simple Analogy
LAN = Roads within your neighborhood.
WAN = Highways connecting different cities and countries.

2) LAN vs WAN

    Feature	                    LAN (Local Area Network)	                        WAN (Wide Area Network)
 Coverage Area	            Small area (home, office, school)	                Large area (cities, countries, worldwide)
 Speed	                    Usually faster	                                    Usually slower than LAN
 Ownership	                Typically owned by one organization or person	    Often uses infrastructure from telecom providers
 Cost	                    Lower cost	                                        Higher cost
 Examples	                Home Wi-Fi network, office network	                The Internet, corporate networks connecting multiple offices
